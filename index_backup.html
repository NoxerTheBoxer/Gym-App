Failure Rules
Never to failure — compounds
Squat, deadlift, bench, barbell row, weighted pull-up. CNS takes 48–72 hrs to recover. Leave 2–3 reps in reserve always.
1–2 reps in reserve — secondary compounds
Work hard but stop before true failure. Maximum stimulus with manageable fatigue. Sweet spot for most of your training.
Last set to failure — isolations only
Tibialis raise, face pull, lateral raise, calf raise, hammer curl. Fatigue here doesn't carry over to anything important.
Golden rule
Form breaks down — set is over. Failure = muscular failure with perfect form. A rounded back deadlift is an injury, not a rep.
